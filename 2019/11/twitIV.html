Sentiment Analysis

John Manalastas | 16 Nov 2019

Market sentiment analysis is used to identify attiudes in text data about the subject of interest.

It is scored between 1 to -1; values closer to 1 is positive, 0 for neatural, and -1 for negative sentiment.

We'll use the textblob pacakge to calc. the polarity value of each tweet on our subject and plot it to a histogram to identify the overall sentiment towards the subject of interest.
