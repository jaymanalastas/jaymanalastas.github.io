Recommendation System

John Manalastas | 30 Oct 2019

In this blog, we'll explore recommendation system. It is using correlations of features to predict top choices for a user.

We'll create a recommendations system using item similarity for our project.

The two most common types of recommender systems are Content-Based and Collaborative Filtering (CF).

  • CF produces recommendations based on the knowledge of users' attitude to items, that it uses the 'wisdom of the crowd' to recommend items.
  • Content - based recommender systems focus on the attributes of the items and give you recommendations based on the similarty between them.

We will focus in on Content-Based recommendation system.
